    Logging In

    Learn how to log in to the POS system, understand till assignment, and troubleshoot common login issues.

    Login Screen Overview

    The login screen is your entry point to the POS system. When you open the application, you will see:

    • Username field - Enter your assigned username
    • Password field - Enter your password (characters are hidden for security)
    • Login button - Click to authenticate and access the system
    • Language selector - Switch between English and Italian in the top-right corner
    • Till information - Displays the name of the till assigned to this device

    Entering Your Credentials

    To log in to the POS system:

    1. Enter your username - Tap or click on the username field and type your assigned username
    2. Enter your password - Tap or click on the password field and type your password
    3. Click Login - Press the Login button or tap Enter on the virtual keyboard
    Using the Virtual Keyboard
    On touchscreen devices, tapping an input field automatically opens the virtual keyboard. You can use either the virtual keyboard or a physical keyboard to enter your credentials. The keyboard provides a full QWERTY layout for text entry.

    Understanding Till Assignment

    Each POS device must be assigned to a specific till (cash register). The till name is displayed on the login screen below the title.

    Till Status Indicators

    • White text - The till is properly configured and ready for use
    • Red text - No till is assigned to this device; only administrators can log in

    For Administrators: Selecting a Till

    If no till is assigned and you are an administrator, you will see the Till Setup screen after logging in:

    1. Review the list of available tills
    2. Click on the till name to select it
    3. The system will assign this device to the selected till

    Troubleshooting Login Errors

    If you encounter problems logging in, check the following solutions:

    Error Message Cause Solution
    Invalid credentials Incorrect username or password Check your credentials for typos. Ensure caps lock is off. Try again carefully.
    Till not configured No till assigned to this device Contact your administrator to assign a till to this device before you can log in.
    Missing credentials Username or password field is empty Fill in both the username and password fields before clicking Login.
    Server error Backend server is not responding Check your network connection. Contact technical support if the problem persists.
    Login failed General authentication failure Verify your credentials and try again. If the problem continues, contact your administrator.

    Session Management

    Staying Logged In

    Once logged in, your session remains active until you explicitly log out. The system maintains your login state even if you refresh the page or temporarily lose network connectivity.

    Logging Out

    To log out of the POS system:

    1. Locate your username displayed in the order panel (right side of the screen)
    2. Click the Logout button next to your name
    3. You will be returned to the login screen
    Tip
    Always log out when leaving the POS terminal unattended. This prevents unauthorized transactions and ensures proper accountability for all orders.

    Security Best Practices

    • Never share your password - Each staff member should have their own account
    • Log out when leaving - Always log out when stepping away from the terminal
    • Report suspicious activity - Tell your administrator immediately if you notice anything unusual
    • Change passwords regularly - Ask your administrator to update your password periodically
    • Check your username - Verify that your name appears in the order panel after logging in

    User Roles

    The POS system has two types of users:

    Role Description Additional Features
    Admin Full system access for managers and supervisors Admin Panel, User Management, Reports, Settings, Till Setup
    Cashier Standard access for front-of-house staff POS operations, taking orders, processing payments

    Administrators will see an Admin button in the top-right corner of the main POS screen, which provides access to the management panel.
