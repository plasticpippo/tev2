    Processing Payments

    Learn how to complete transactions, handle tips, and process different payment methods.

    Opening the Payment Modal

    When an order is ready to be paid:

    1. Ensure all items are added to the order
    2. Review the totals in the order panel
    3. Click the Payment button
    4. The payment modal opens with a complete order summary

    Payment Modal Overview

    The payment modal displays important information:

    Order Information

    • Table assignment (if applicable)
    • List of items with quantities and prices
    • Subtotal (before tax)
    • Tax amount
    • Tip amount
    • Total amount due

    Tip Section

    • - button to decrease tip
    • Current tip amount display
    • + button to increase tip

    Payment Method Buttons

    • Pay with Cash (green button)
    • Pay with Card (blue button)

    Adding Tips

    To add a tip to the order:

    1. In the payment modal, locate the tip section
    2. Click the + button to increase tip in small increments
    3. Click the - button to decrease tip
    4. The tip is added to the total automatically
    Tip
    Hold down the +/- buttons to quickly adjust the tip amount. The amount updates in real-time.

    Selecting Payment Method

    The POS supports two payment methods:

    Cash Payment

    • Click the green Pay with Cash button
    • Record the cash amount received from the customer
    • Calculate and provide change as needed
    • Transaction is recorded as a cash payment

    Card Payment

    • Click the blue Pay with Card button
    • Process the card through your payment terminal
    • Wait for payment authorization
    • Transaction is recorded as a card payment
    Important
    The POS does not process card payments directly. Use your external payment terminal for card transactions. Select "Card" in the POS to record that the payment was made via card.

    Completing the Transaction

    To finalize payment:

    1. Review the final total (including tax and tip)
    2. Select the appropriate payment method
    3. Complete the payment outside the POS if using card
    4. Click the payment button to confirm
    5. The transaction is saved to the system
    6. The order panel clears for the next order
    Success
    Once payment is completed, the transaction is recorded and can be viewed in Transaction History by administrators.

    Understanding Tax Calculation

    Tax is automatically calculated based on your establishment's settings:

    Tax Modes

    • Tax Inclusive - Prices shown include tax
    • Tax Exclusive - Tax is added on top of prices
    • No Tax - No tax is applied

    The tax amount displayed in the payment modal reflects the configuration set by your administrator.

    Receipt Information

    After completing a payment:

    • The transaction is recorded in the system
    • Administrators can view transaction details in the Transaction History
    • Transaction records include: date, time, items, amounts, payment method, user
