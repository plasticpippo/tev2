    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about using the POS system. Questions are organized by topic to help you find what you need quickly.

    Getting Started

    How do I log in for the first time?

    Your administrator will provide you with a username and password. On the login screen, enter your credentials and click the Login button. If your device hasn't been assigned to a Till, you'll see an error message asking you to contact your administrator.

    See also: Logging In

    What if my device shows "Till not configured"?

    This means your device hasn't been assigned to a till. Contact your administrator to assign your device to a till in the Till Management section. Once assigned, you'll be able to log in and use the POS.

    How do I change the language?

    Click on the language switcher in the header (EN/IT) to toggle between English and Italian. The language preference is saved for your session. Both the interface and error messages will be displayed in the selected language.

    Orders and Payments

    How do I add items to an order?

    Tap on product buttons in the product grid to add items to your current order. Each tap adds one unit of that product variant. To add multiple units quickly, tap the button multiple times or use the quantity controls in the order panel.

    See also: Taking Orders

    How do I remove an item from an order?

    In the order panel, tap the minus (-) button next to the item to reduce its quantity. When the quantity reaches zero, the item is removed. Alternatively, use the Clear button to remove all items from the order.

    Note: Removed items are logged in the Order Activity Log for audit purposes.

    Can I undo a deleted item?

    No, once an item is removed from an order, it cannot be automatically restored. You will need to re-add the item by tapping the product button again. The removal is logged in the Order Activity Log.

    How do I add a tip to a payment?

    In the payment modal, use the tip buttons (0%, 5%, 10%, 15%, 20%) or enter a custom tip amount. The tip is added to the total and recorded in the transaction. Tips are included in the daily closing totals.

    See also: Processing Payments

    What payment methods are supported?

    The POS supports two payment methods:

    • Cash: For cash payments, the system shows the amount due and can calculate change.
    • Card: For card payments, the system records the transaction. Actual card processing is handled by your external card terminal.

    Tables and Tabs

    How do I assign an order to a table?

    Tap the Table button in the order panel to open the table assignment modal. Select a room, then tap on an available table to assign the order. The table status will update to "Occupied."

    See also: Managing Tables

    How do I transfer an order to another table?

    Open the table assignment modal and select a different table. The order will be reassigned, and both table statuses will update accordingly. If the destination table already has an open tab, you'll need to close or transfer that tab first.

    What is a tab and when should I use one?

    A Tab is a saved order that can be loaded and modified later. Use tabs for:

    • Customers who may add more items over time (e.g., bar patrons)
    • Orders that need to be paid for later
    • Splitting bills across multiple payments

    See also: Working with Tabs

    How do I transfer items between tabs?

    Open the Tab Manager and select the source tab. Use the Transfer Items option to move selected items to another tab. This is useful when customers want to split a bill or when items were added to the wrong tab.

    Account and Security

    How do I reset a forgotten password?

    Contact your administrator to reset your password. Admin users can change passwords from the User Management section. For security reasons, there is no self-service password reset option.

    What should I do if I see "Session has expired"?

    Your login session has timed out for security. Simply log in again with your credentials. Your current order is automatically saved and will be restored when you log back in.

    How do I log out?

    Tap the logout button in the header. Your current order is saved and will be available when you (or another user) log in next. If you have an active order, it will be preserved in your order session.

    Technical Issues

    What happens if the network goes down?

    The POS system requires a network connection to function. If the network goes down:

    • You won't be able to process new orders or payments
    • Existing saved tabs will be available once connectivity is restored
    • Data entered during the outage may not be saved

    Contact your IT support or administrator to resolve network issues.

    Why is my screen not responding to touch?

    If the touchscreen is not responding:

    • Check if the browser window is focused (click anywhere on the screen)
    • Try refreshing the page (press F5 or Ctrl+R)
    • Check if the virtual keyboard is blocking touch events
    • Verify your touchscreen drivers are properly installed

    If problems persist, contact your administrator.

    Why are my changes not appearing?

    If changes made on another device are not visible:

    • Refresh the page using Ctrl+R or the refresh button
    • Log out and log back in to force a data refresh
    • Check your network connection

    Some data may take a few seconds to sync across devices.

    What does "Too many requests" mean?

    This error appears when you've made too many actions in a short time. The system has rate limiting to prevent abuse. Wait a few seconds and try again. If the problem persists, contact your administrator.

    Administrator Questions

    How do I add a new user?

    Go to User Management in the Admin Panel. Click "Add User" and fill in the required information (username, password, role). Choose between Admin (full access) or Cashier (POS only) roles.

    How do I set up a new till?

    Go to Till Management in the Admin Panel. Click "Add Till" and enter a name for the till. To assign a device, use the device assignment feature or have the user log in from that device.

    How do I close the business day?

    Go to Daily Closing in the Admin Panel. Review the current day's totals and click "Close Day" to generate the closing report. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

    How do I customize the product grid layout?

    Product grid layouts can be customized in Product Management. Select a category and till, then drag and drop products to arrange them. Changes are saved automatically.
