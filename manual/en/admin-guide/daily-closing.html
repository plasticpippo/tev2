    Daily Closing

    The Daily Closing feature allows you to end the business day and generate comprehensive reports. This guide explains how to close the business day, understand the generated reports, and handle special situations.

    Understanding Business Day

    A business day is the operational period for your restaurant. Unlike calendar days, business days are configured to match your establishment's operating hours.

    Business Day Characteristics

    • Starts automatically at a configured time (e.g., 6:00 AM)
    • Continues until manually closed or the next auto-start time
    • All transactions within the period are grouped together
    • Sales totals reset when a new business day begins

    Configuring Business Day

    Business day settings can be configured in the Settings section:

    • Auto Start Time: When a new business day automatically begins
    • Manual Closing: Whether to require manual end of day

    Closing the Current Business Day

    To close the business day and generate a closing report:

    1. Navigate to Daily Closing in the Admin Panel
    2. Click Close Business Day
    3. Review the closing summary (see below)
    4. Click Confirm Closing to finalize

    Viewing Closing History

    Access previous daily closing reports:

    1. Navigate to Daily Closing in Admin Panel
    2. View the list of previous closings
    3. Click on a closing to view details

    Reopening a Closed Day

    Once a business day is closed, it cannot be formally reopened through the system. However, there are options if you need to make corrections:

    Options for Corrections

    • Manual Records: Reference any external records you maintain
    • Transaction History: Individual transactions are still viewable in the Transactions section
    • Next Day Adjustment: Account for discrepancies in the next day's closing
    Best Practice: Always review the closing summary carefully before confirming. Ensure all transactions have been processed and all staff have completed their shifts.

    Best Practices

    Before Closing

    • Verify all transactions are complete
    • Ensure all staff have logged out
    • Check for any open tabs that need attention
    • Confirm all tills are settled

    After Closing

    • Print or export the closing report
    • Reconcile with any external records
    • File reports for accounting purposes
    • Address any discrepancies before the next day

    Timing

    • Close at a consistent time each day
    • Consider end-of-day procedures when setting auto-start time
    • Allow time for the closing process before the next auto-start
    Tip: Schedule closing for a time when business is typically slow to avoid interruptions.
