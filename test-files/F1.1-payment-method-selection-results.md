## UI/UX Observations

### Strengths
1. **Clear Visual Distinction:** Green for cash, blue for card - easy to differentiate
2. **Consistent Styling:** Both buttons have identical size, padding, and typography
3. **Accessible Design:** High contrast white text on colored backgrounds
4. **Responsive Layout:** Buttons use flex-1, adapting to screen width
5. **Hover Feedback:** Both buttons have hover states (lighter shades)

### Areas for Improvement
1. **No Processing Indicator:** No loading spinner or "Processing..." message during payment
2. **No Confirmation Feedback:** No "Payment successful" toast or message after payment
3. **No Receipt Prompt:** No option to print or email receipt after payment
4. **No Payment Icons:** Buttons use text only - could benefit from cash/card icons
5. **No Other Payment Methods:** Only Cash and Card available - no mobile payments, split payments, etc.

### Recommendations
1. Add visual processing indicator (spinner) during payment
2. Show success confirmation message after payment completion
3. Consider adding payment method icons for better visual recognition
4. Add receipt generation options post-payment
5. Consider additional payment methods (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Split payment)
